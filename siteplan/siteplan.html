Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to display details and information about Gauley River Expeditions, a rafting adventure company located on the Gauley river is West Virginia. The site will provide details about upcoming expeditions and how perspective patrons can register to attend.

Target Audience

The website's target audience will be adults looking for activities for themselves and/or their older teenage children. Site users will enjoy outdoor activities and consider themselves adventurous individuals. Most users will love in the Eastern United States and have income to spend on vacations and leasure activities.

Physical Descriptions

  1. Age: Typically ages 16+, The mahirity will be adults ages 20-45
  2. Gender: All genders
  3. Education: No educationaly requirements
  4. Economic Status: individuals with descretionary income
  5. Personal Interests: Outdoors, Aventure Seekers, Adrenaline Junkies
  6. Language: English
  7. Geographic Location: Eastern United States

Technical Descriptions

  1. Devices: Desktop/Laptop comuters, tables, smartphones
  2. Operating System: No OS restrictions
  3. Browsers: Google Chroms, Mozilla Firefox, Internet Explorer/Edge, Safari
  4. Rate of Visit: A few times a year; Mostly during the summer/fall months
  5. Web Use: Those comfortable browsing and receiving information from the internet

Scenarios

  1. Where are you located?
    • We are located in Lansing, West Virgina.
  2. Who long are your river expeditions?
    • We offer rafting adventures between 4 and 24 hours.
  3. What ages are recommended for your expeditions?
    • Lower Gauley expeditions are recommed for ages 8 and up. Upper Gauley expeidtions are recommed for 16 and up.
  4. What safety equipment is provided?
    • We provide rafting helmets and life jackets. Life jackets must be warn at all times.
  5. How cold is the water?
    • On average the water temperate is between 50-65 degrees. Wet suites are recommended and can be rented for a nominal fee.
