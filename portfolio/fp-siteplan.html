Site Purpose

A personal portfolio website for BYU Idaho student Jeremiah Astin. The site will showcase web design and development projects showcasing work in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL. The site will also include a personal resume page and a contact email form.

Target Audience

The main target audience will be potential freelance clients and/or website design and development firms looking to hire recent graduates.

Physical Descriptions

  1. Age:The majority will be adults ages 30-50
  2. Gender: All genders
  3. Education: Those with a background in website design and development.
  4. Economic Status:Business professionals
  5. Personal Interests: Website Design/Development, Coding
  6. Language: English
  7. Geographic Location: No Geographic Location

Technical Descriptions

  1. Devices: Desktop/Laptop computers, tables, smartphones
  2. Operating System: No OS restrictions
  3. Browsers: Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Internet Explorer/Edge, Safari
  4. Rate of Visit: 2-3 Times
  5. Web Use: Those comfortable working with computers.

Scenarios

  1. What did you study at BYU Idaho?
    • Applied Technologies with an emphasis in Web Site Design/Development and Database.
  2. Can I view your resume?
    • A link will be provided to a downloadable resume.
  3. How can I contact you?
    • Complete the contact me email form.
  4. Where do you currently live?
    • Dayton, Ohio
  5. What is your favorite programming language?
    • HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Site Map

Home Page: Provide an introduction to the site and myself.

Resume: A page to view a responsive version of my resume, as well as a link to download a pdf copy of the resume.

Contact: An email form to contact me with questions.

Portfolio: A page to provide links to websites I have designed and worked on as a student.

Typography
